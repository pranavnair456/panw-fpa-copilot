# Executive Brief — Palo Alto Networks, FY2026Q3

**Headline.** Total revenue landed at **$3,002M**, $+59M (+2.0%) versus the guidance midpoint of $2,943M — a favorable result.

**The variance story.** The headline beat is mostly inorganic: $388M came from the CyberArk and Chronosphere acquisitions. Against our model's organic forecast of $2,564M, the underlying business beat by just $+50M — acquisitions account for 89% of the total upside to forecast. Read against guidance (which already embedded the deals), the $+59M beat reflects organic execution, not the acquisitions themselves.

**Drivers.** Remaining performance obligations (backlog) rose to **$18,400M** (+15% Q/Q), but $1,800M of that increase is acquired — only $600M is organic, consistent with the modest organic revenue beat.

**Forecast.** Our backtested model projects FY2026Q4 organic revenue of **$2,805M** (80% interval $2,736-$2,875M).

**What to watch.** Whether organic NGS ARR growth re-accelerates as the acquisitions annualize, and how cleanly the acquired revenue converts the elevated backlog into recognized revenue over the next 2-3 quarters.
